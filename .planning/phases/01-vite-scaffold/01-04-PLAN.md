## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-04-01 | Tampering | cart.add() via ProductCard | accept | In Phase 1, PRODUCTS data is hardcoded — price and sku cannot be manipulated by the user. | T-04-02 | Tampering | window.cart global removed | mitigate | Removing the window.cart global prevents browser console injection of cart mutations (e.g., `window.cart.add({sku:'x', price:0})`) — all cart mutations now go through the CartContext reducer |
| T-04-03 | Information Disclosure | PRODUCTS/CLASSES as ES module exports | accept | Product catalog data (names, prices, images) is intentionally public — it is displayed to all visitors |
